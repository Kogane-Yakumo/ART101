Part 1: Pseudocode an everyday task

Buying Groceries:

  1. Check the fridge and/or pantry for what is running out.
  2. Create a grocery list.
  3. Leave the house with your grocery list and a means of payment.
  4. Travel to your preferred market.
  5. Get a cart if necessary.
  6. Locate an item on your grocery list.
  7. Find a good variation of the item on your list.
  8. Repeat the previous two steps until you have every item on your list.
  9. Head to checkout and pay for your groceries.
  10. Bag your groceries.
  11. Return your cart if you got one.
  12. Return home with your groceries.
